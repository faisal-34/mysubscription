Common questions

Everything you need to know before subscribing.

What counts as a "content update"?
Anything you'd want changed on the site — text edits, new photos, event listings, schedule changes, new pages, or form updates. If you're unsure, just ask.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. Cancel from your Stripe customer portal before your next billing date and you won't be charged again. No fees, no lock-in.
What if I need more updates than my plan allows?
You can upgrade your plan anytime, or I'll quote additional requests as one-off work at a fair rate. I'll always tell you before doing anything outside the plan.
Is my payment information secure?
All payments are processed by Stripe — the same platform used by Amazon and Google. Your card details are never stored on my servers.
